Like a proper pint? Then pull up a stool at The Old Royal. Our traditional bar serves a great selection of drinks at prices that are easy to swallow - and that also go down well with a nice bit of pub-food if you fancy it. If you're into cask ales, you'll be pleased to know that every one of our great British brews is given the love and attention you'd expect from a Cask Marque accredited pub in Birmingham. So whether it's a sociable pint, a celebratory pint or a crafty after-work pint, at The Old Royal you can be sure that it'll be a perfectly poured one at that.
