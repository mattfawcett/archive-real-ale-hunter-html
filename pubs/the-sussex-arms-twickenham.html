An astonishing array of beers (and ciders) nearly all of which are from independent brewers. The list continually changes with no obvious permanent beers. 16 different ales on my last visit - English, Scottish, Welsh, you name it and 2 ciders, all from hand pumps. With another 20+ in bottles. Quite outstanding and well worth a visit.

Added by The Umpire - almost 7 years ago

12 beers and 3 ciders available, from which we chose to concentrate upon: Maverick from Fyne Ales, Magic Rock's Curious, Three Tuns Golden Spicy Bitter, Black Eel by Twickenham and finally, Dark Star's American Pale Ale. That said, we weren't halfway through what was on offer. Great beer, great pub, we'll be back!
