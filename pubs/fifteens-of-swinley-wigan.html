Fifteens of Swinley is a pub and stylish B&B situated on Upper Dicconson Street, just a few minutes walk from Wigan town centre and very close to the train stations. If you are looking for a good place to drink with a great, friendly atmosphere come and check Fifteens of Swinley out. Fifteens is the ideal place to stay for a weekend visit to Wigan as it is just 5 minutes walk away from all the main clubs and bars, also an ideal place to stay for midweek work commitments. Continental breakfast is included and free Wi-fi is available. Pub opening times: Sun - Thurs 12pm - 11pm Fri & Sat - 12pm - 1am We now have a pool table and darts board!!
