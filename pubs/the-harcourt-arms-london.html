While The Harcourt Arms situated just off the Marylebone Road is described as a 'Typical English Pub' it is in fact anything but. It's usually known as 'the Swedish Pub' and does indeed sit within a small Swedish community. The atmosphere is warm and relaxing, while the staff often appear delightfully flaky. It offers an interesting range of beers, as well as Swedish cider, spirits and a mix of English and Swedish food.

Added by BENNETTONBEER - over 7 years ago

Visited the Harcourt Arms on the 2/6/2011. It had two Real Ales on and both were in excellent condition. Also on draught were seven Lagers, plus Guinness and Aspalls cider. This is a favourite watering hole for me on the way back to Paddington station, and its not let me down yet. Well worth a visit.
