The Victoria is a Mitchells pub at Glasson Dock near Lancaster.The pub is very clean,and is divided into three parts ,diningroom gamesroom and a nice bar lounge.Food is available from alarge menu,plus there is a specials board. There are four handpumps on the bar,and beers are chosen from a list supplied by Mitchells Brewery who own the pub,but no longer brew beer.
