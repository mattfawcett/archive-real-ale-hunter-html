The Bromsgrove Unionist Club. This is a Members only Club although guests are welcome including card carrying members of CAMRA. Membership is available on request at the Club. Situated on the Kidderminster Road on the junction with Church Road and opposite Sander Park. The Club offer a relaxing atmosphere within its own large grounds. Good cask beers always available including Bathams and an ever changing guest. The Club has Marstons Ales including Banks's Bitter and Mild, Theakstons Traditional Cider and a selection of Lagers and bottled beers. The facilities include 3 snooker tables, dartboard, dominoes and cards. Entertainment is on a Saturday night on a fortnightly basis and a quiz is held on the first Thursday of the month. For more information vist the website.
