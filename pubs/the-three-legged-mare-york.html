This is one of the 3 pubs owned by York Brewery in the city. It not only has a fine selection of cask ales but also serves a wide range of lagers, spirits, ciders and wines. The beers go as fast as they come on so make sure you make the most of its excellent selection! Food is served 12 to 9pm.
