Added by matt - 2008

This pub is very similar decor to Dr. Okells in Leeds. It has the same pumps, but has twice as many offering a wide variety of ales, continental beers and fruit beers on tap. It was very busy, but there was seating at the back and around the pub. I had a nice half pint of frambozen on tap. My favourite pub in Liverpool so far, highly recommended if you appreciate an extensive, high quality range of ales and beers.
