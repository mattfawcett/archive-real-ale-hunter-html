Whitelocks is hidden up an alley of Briggate, the main shopping street in Leeds. There is quite a large seating area outside and this pub is always in the Good Beer Guide. they have up to 4 guests on at a time and quite often have brews from York brewery. They server Food 12 - 2.30 and 5-7 in the restaurant area and bar snacks 12 - 7.
