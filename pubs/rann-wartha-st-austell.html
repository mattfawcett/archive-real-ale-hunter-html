The Rann Wartha is part of J D Wetherspoon Plc trading as Lloyds No 1 bar. Food served all day from 9am to 10pm with some themed food nights usual of JDW. 10 hand pumps with a selection of real ale both local and national. As part of a company wide campaign, the Rann Wartha hosts several beer and wine festivals through out the year as well as organising it's own with local brewers. There is also a live Dj every Friday and Saturday evening from 9pm.
