This pub is said to be the oldest pub in Lancaster.The pub is tucked away in a small alley near to the bus station,and is made up of a lounge bar and a dining room.The pub is owned by Mitchells of Lancaster who provide the cask beers they buy from independent breweries. The manager orders from their list. The pub is open 12.00 till 12.00.Food is on sale from 12,00to 3.00,and5.00 till 8.00. 12.00 till 6.00 on sunday.
