Chaplin’s award-winning independent bar, restaurant and live music venue. The Cellar Bar, Chaplin’s Bar and our wonderful south-facing garden are the best in Bournemouth. If you’re looking for a luxurious setting to enjoy a great cocktail or a gravity poured real ale, enjoy some fine cuisine in a beautiful restaurant, at reasonable prices or want to catch some great up-and-coming music acts, it's the place to be. Chaplin's is based in the heart of Boscombe, 5 minutes walk from the seafront and close to the Victorian shopping arcade and the O2 Academy. A lot of thought and attention has been put into creating a unique environment for people to unwind and have fun. It’s a great place to meet people from all walks of life. • 2 level Pub with a traditional pub with music stage downstairs and a wine-bar/cocktail/high-end spirits and food premises above. • Bournemouth in Bloom Gold Award winning garden with sheltered areas and balconies. • 7 days a week original live music and 24 hr license. • Recently won National Best Bar None award at the House of Lords with particular note of its role as a community pub 2012-2013. • Serves Real Ale from Gravity pour and hand pump currently rotating between 10 local breweries, with sunny republic ans sixpenny featured permanently., Also serving some local ciders.. • Built from reclaimed and recycled materials, serving local food with some organic ingredients. Owners hope to set up a small brewery on the site in the near future. • Quirky and unique definitely worth a visit.
