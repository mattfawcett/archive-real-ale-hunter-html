The Castle Inn is a traditional family-run 16th Century thatched free house pub and hotel with an extensive food menu and regularly changing local real ales, near the beautiful Lulworth Cove & Durdle Door of the Jurassic Coastline and surrounded by the beautiful Isle of Purbeck countryside. Dogs are very welcome throughout the Castle Inn. A large selection of local and regional Real Ales, all served by gravity direct from the cask. Website:
