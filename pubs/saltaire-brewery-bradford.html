This brewery is open for visits, which you can book via the website or giving them a ring. The bar area can also be booked for parties and other events. The real ale society of Leeds University visited the brewery on 13/2/07, and was pleasantly surprised by the standard and quality of the beer, staff and surroundings. Being a relatively new build, Saltaire brewery is of innovative design and it's attention to detail excellent. The brewery is well equiped with a visitor center, 4 pumped bar (beer direct from the conditioning room) and bar seating area all completed to a high standard.
