The George is located on St Thomas's Road in Chorley. We are the friendliest pub in town! Whether you just fancy a quiet pint in the week or a late weekend dancing the night away The George is definitely for you. Get down and have a beer with our world famous manager Paul Higgins! All of our staff are very friendly and helpful. Come in from the cold and warm up on our open coal fire!
