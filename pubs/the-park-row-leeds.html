The Park Row Bar and Brasserie on the street level features a newly revamped lounge and dining area which serves the pub classics with a gourmet twist, with the added bonus you can get a pieminister pie to go with your pint. The sophisticated atmosphere and high quality range of spirits, wines and beers, including 6 locally brewed cask ales makes this bar a perfect occasion for business lunches. The atmosphere seamlessly translates to the evening setting with a regular pianist serenading an after work cocktail, as the night progresses you can dance the night away with the DJ playing until 2am.
