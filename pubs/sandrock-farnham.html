Added by Cambelt - over 7 years ago

I went to the Sandrock in expectation of finding the fabulous range of Black Country beers it was famous for. What a dissappointment. Not only was there no Bathams, Holdens, etc. but the beers were all "mainstream" and had come from Enterprise Inns' main list. There was a lot attention to food, I found, and not on the selection of ales. What a shame.
