A poor selection of ales were available at the Victoria on this visit. Just Tetleys and Landlord were the only regulars and the rather strong 6% Gorlovka was the only guest, which I had a pint of. The lack of ale here is probably due to the pubco Mitchells & Butlers enforcing a beer list containing just beers from the bigger breweries on the pub, also happening at the Scarbrough and Palace in Leeds.
