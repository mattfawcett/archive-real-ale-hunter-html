Added by Big dug - 2010

Arrived early evening and was faced with the backs of 4 hefty locals who made no attempt to to move so that I could see what beers were available. The welcome from the bar staff was a bit lacklustre too. All this coupled with a uninspiring decor and smokers near the back door with smoke drifting in meant a rather dispiriting experience. Beer was ok but nothing to write home about or indeed here.
