This pub regularly wins Camras pub of the year competition and is easily one of the best pubs in Bradford. They have a very extensive range of hand pulled ales and Foreign beers both on tap and in bottles. They feature in the Good beer guide and also in the Good beer guide Belgium edition in the bottled beer section. The pub gets very busy at weekends.
