Added by Big dug - 2010

Tenant-run Harveys pub in the town centre. Clientele consists mainly of older, serious drinkers so no crowds of alcopop-fuelled youths shouting and glaring at each other. Staff are friendly and efficient and are good at keeping the punters area clean. There are usually three Harveys beers on; often the Mild although I'm not sure they sell enough of it all the time to ensure great condition. I had a pint of Best Bitter and that was on top form. There aren't many sessions bitters better than Harveys when it's this good.
