Tribute and Deuchars IPA are the permanent beers, with a weekly change of guest ale, often two guests in the summer, from a number of well known and local breweries. Timothy Taylor, Wychwood Hobgoblin, Tring Brewery, Concrete Cow and Whittlebury Brewery are most common on the guest pumps. The riverside pub with Real Ales has great British food and a friendly atmosphere. The open fire and a seperate stove warm the comfortably seated front room, with the beer garden backing onto the River Great Ouse. Disabled access is provided.
