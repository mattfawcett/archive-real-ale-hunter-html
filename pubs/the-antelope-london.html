This is an excellent Fullers pub, full of tradition(with its own Cricket team for instance)and charm. Has a range of good ales and first class pub food. Cosy downstairs and with a dining area and private rooms upstairs. Nestling in a side street not far from Sloane Square underground station it is easy to reach and well worth a visit.

Added by BENNETTONBEER - over 7 years ago

Visited this Fullers pub yet again on the 22/6/2011. It has become one of my favourite watering-holes in Belgravia and has yet to let me down. Always worth a visit, though perhaps avoiding lunchtime as half of Chelsea and Belgravia appears to arrive demanding food, particularly their Fish & Chips. The beer is from the Fullers range with guests and is kept really well.
