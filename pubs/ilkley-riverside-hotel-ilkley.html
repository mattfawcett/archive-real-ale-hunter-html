If you are looking for a warm welcome, friendly atmosphere & a picturesque riverside setting, The 'Ilkley Riverside Hotel' really does provide all three in abundance. The hotel is only a few minutes from the bustling Ilkley centre, yet set in the tranquil riverside gardens away from all the noise. Escaping to Ilkley Moor & the Dales is very easy indeed, with the Dalesway track starting only 100 yards away at the Pack Horse bridge over the River Wharfe.
