Website: The Groes Wen Inn is well known in Penhow and across Monmouthshire as a fine country pub. A popular pub restaurant famous for great food, we are a short distance from Penhow Castle, the oldest inhabited castle in Wales. There is always a warm welcome with roaring log fires, freshly prepared food, a great selection of real ales and a diverse wine menu for you to choose from.
