Added by garth - almost 12 years ago

Walked up to this pub from the house we were staying at. A pint of Jennings Bitter and then a Cumberland followed, great tasty beer. We then ordered food, I got the mixed grill, I nearly fell out my chair when it arrived, the portion size was huge. I'm not a 'small' person by any means, so I'm embarassed to say I couldn't finish it. Defininately for the hungrier bloke. Quality of food and the service was excellent. Well recommended.
