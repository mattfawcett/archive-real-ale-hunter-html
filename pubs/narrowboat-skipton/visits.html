Another good visit to the Narrowboat,they never let you down.There was seven cask beers on the bar four of which i had never seen before.I had a pint of Stirling which i liked and then we had Dents Frosty Weather for which i gave top marks, would very much liked to have stayed longer,but we are limited for time and two more bars to visit before our return to Fleetwood.
