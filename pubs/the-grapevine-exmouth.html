An excellent addition to Exmouth real ale pubs, albeit on the pricey side for a pint. Cask beers rotated regularly, I've sampled pints from Darkstar, Bath Ales, Hunters, Otter, Skinners and others here. Also a wide selection of Belgian beers and local (Green Valley) and national cider. The food is very good and reasonable, (Â£5 for mussels & chips is a winner in my book). Clientle is generally 25+, and decent live music on Fridays makes this a pub with a really good atmosphere.

Added by BENNETTONBEER - 2013

Visited this superb Bistro-Pub on the 16/5/2012. This has to be one of my all time favourite drinking dens with excellent food, friendly service and a very wide selection of draught and bottled British and European Beers. The Fry's Real Ale was in particularly fine condition, while the Bavarian Schneider Weiss was a dream. This place is a must visit!
