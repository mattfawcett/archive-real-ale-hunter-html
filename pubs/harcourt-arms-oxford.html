Fine Fuller's pub serving the best-kept Chiswick and London Pride in Oxford. Log fires in the winter, the best (and cheapest) toasted sandwiches in town, a good mix of locals, students and staff from the nearby university press, this pub has hardly altered in all the years I've been drinking here. And that can only be a blessing in these days of free wi-fi, Sky TV, continental lagers, Guinness Extra Cold and gastro-fare. God bless The Harcourt Arms!
