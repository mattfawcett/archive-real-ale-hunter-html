Wonderfull local pub set right in the the middle of poulton square. We have 4 rotating guest ales and various other beers on draught. We have live music most saturday nights with very good local bands. We show live football, and horse racing is on all day, every day. We are a dog friendly pub and everyone is welcome in this traditional gem of a pub.

Excellent pub. Very friendly staff and very warming atmosphere. Live sport on the various tv's and very big support for Blackpool fc. When I was in it was the aftermath of a blackpool match being shown live and the place was buzzing. Would recommend this place to anyone who likes a great pint surrounded by great people. Well worth a visit.
