CAMRA PUB OF THE YEAR 2013 CALDERDALE AND HALIFAX BRANCH The Masons Arms reopened it's doors 4th April 2012 after much needed refurbishments. It now has 2 log burning fires lots more seating. There are 5 handpulls offering a variety of local real ales. The pub has a jukebox, books, a variety of games and daily newspapers available. Quiz night is on a Wednesday, it's free to enter. The staff are always chatty and welcoming. Overall this is a good local pub with top beers just what most of us want. Dog are welcome!!

Added by BENJO - 2009

We spent sunday on the Lancs Yorkshire border and called at the Masons Arms in Todmorden.this is one of the few Copper Dragon owned pubs.They had three of there own brews on plus one guest which was Bank Top Flat Cap. I had a pint of flat cap and then we went on Golden Pippin which was the beer we had come here for. All the beers were excellent and along with a first class sunday roast dinner for five pounds, top marks to the Masons Arms we are certain to be back.
