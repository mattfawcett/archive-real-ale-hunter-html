The Steamer is a large pub on the seafront at Fleetwood.The pub serves five cask beers with Wells Bombardier being the regular beer. Alongside that there are four guests beers,at the present time they are Tiger,Old Trip, Nimmos xxxx and Moorhouses Blond Witch.The landlord puts on good entertainment and food is available during the day. There is a pool table and a snooker table which are well used.
