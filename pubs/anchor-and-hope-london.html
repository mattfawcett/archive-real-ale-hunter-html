Traditional East-End corner pub. Very small, just one L-shaped bar. No food, but beers excellent. Tables and seating outside alongside the Lea Navigation (also some inside!). No car-parking, but since the best way to get there is by bike along the Lea towpath, that doesn't matter. It's 800 yards north of Lea Bridge Road along the towpath. I've accidentally put the marker on the wrong side of the river Lea! A friendly, unspoiled traditional local boozer.
