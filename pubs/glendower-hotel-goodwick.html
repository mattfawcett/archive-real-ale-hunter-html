Situated in the picturesque village of Goodwick, within easy walking distance of Fishguard Harbour, the Glendower Hotel is a friendly family run hotel with a traditional bar and a genuine local atmosphere. The re-opened Goodwick Railway Station is 250m away. All rooms with en-suite, Freeview TV, tea & coffee facilities and free WiFi
