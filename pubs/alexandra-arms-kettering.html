Totals are around 4000 plus in over 5 years, and something like 600 plus brewerys. Opening times, Monday tto Thursday2pm till 11pm, Friday and Saturday 12 till midnight, and 12 -11 on Sunday. Pub also has Northants Skittles and pool table plus TV.
