This is one of the pubs owned by Market Town Taverns - The pub opened in 2003 and was formally an off licence. The number 50 bus stops outside it so it is easily accessible from anyone on the route to Leeds. The pub is no Smoking throughout and food is served both in the bar and the Brasserie upstairs. Serving times are Monday 6 - 9 food served downstairs only, Tuesday to Saturdays 6 -9 food served both upstairs and downstairs and Sundays food is served 12 -5 both up and downstairs. Dogs are welcome and the pub has a dog food menu. Children are also welcome in the bar till 6pm and in the upstairs restaurant. Highchairs & changing facilities available.
