Added by BENJO - 2012

I had arranged to meet a friend in Longridge a village i had only visited once before when iwas in the pub trade and had played bowls at a whitbread pub called The Black Dog.We walked up and down the streets to find every pub shut with the exception of the Townley were we spent the afternoon and had a couple of pints.after five o clock we went to the White Bull as i knew that they had Spitfire a cask that i like.The beer was ok but the best thing about Longridge if you are a cask beer drinker is the great regular bus service which gets you out of the village.
