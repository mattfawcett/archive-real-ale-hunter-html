Housed in a Grade II listed building, on Bradford's most attractive street, The Sparrow is a small 'bier cafe' specialising in the sale of quality beers. On the bar we offer 4 real ales, 2 craft keg beers, 2 vintage ciders and 3 Czech lagers from the 'Bernard' brewery. In the fridges you will find 50+ bottled beers from some of the world's most original brewers including a wide selection from Britain, USA, Belgium & Germany. Select wines and spirits are also on offer. Deli style sandwiches and platters, using quality local produce, along with sweet treats, speciality teas, coffees and juices are available all day. The arts feature heavily at The Sparrow. Our music playlists are hand selected and updated on a regular basis and works by local artists hang on the wall. Keep your eyes peeled for special events. By day The Sparrow is a relaxed place to grab a bite to eat and have a drink. By night it is a place to 'hangout' with a quality beverage against a backdrop of great music and art. Opening Times: Mon - Tues: 11:00 - 20:00 Wed - Thurs: 11:00 - 23:00 Fri - Sat: 11:00 - 00:00 Closed Sundays except Bank Holidays
