This is a pub that you must not miss if you visit Manchester.It is a listed building 120 years old,and has its own brewery.Food is available each day up to eight pm, six pm on sunday. There is always a great choice of cask beers available most from their own brewery but plenty of guests and there is a cider or perry on the bar.
