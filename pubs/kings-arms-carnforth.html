A pub in the small village of Burton.There used to be three pubs in the village, now there is only one.The pub serves food in the evening in a candle lit dineing room,and is said to be very popular with visitors and locals alike, there are three cask beers from the Mitchells guest beers list.I could not fint out the opening hours,but it seems that the pub does not open at lunchtime during the week.
