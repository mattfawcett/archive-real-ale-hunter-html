The Vagabond, a cultured, real pub, situated on the harbourside in the beautiful west coast town of Whitehaven, on the edge of the English Lake District National Park. We serve excellent chef prepared meals at pub prices, including traditional sunday roasts, a vast choice of Real Ales, and some great folk and blues music to enjoy. Frequented by Artists, Musicians, Thespians, all manner of characters, and those demanding an amazing eating experience for a very reasonable price. Daily Specials are offered and changed regularly. We would advise booking in advance on the weekend! Parties from 2 - 100 catered for, Banquets and Hot and Cold Buffets (a la carte)
