We are a village pub located in the centre of Slaithwaite. We have 8 hand pulled ales on and one hand pulled cider from Westons. We have two pumps dedicated to our local brewery Empire which is located on the canal a short walk away from the pub. Commerciale at 3.8% which is a bitter and we also have Moonraker Mild at 3.8%. Our other six pumps have rotating guest beers on at all times, which over the weekend change rapidly. We also have Boddingtons, Guinness, Becks & Staropramen, plus a selection of bottled beers & lagers. Wine is available by the glass or bottle. We are on the Real Ale Trail and are only a short 5 min walk from Slaithwaite Railway Station. We welcome, families, walkers & business people. We have a small function room available for use at no charge.
