This was one of the best pubs that we went to on our Braford trip. The pub is very nicely decorated inside and has a long bar with 8 real ale pumps on offer, which is pretty impressive. I had a pint of Marbles White and then I tried the pubs own Dolly Bitter brewed by Moorhouses which was a very nice brew.
