Opening Mon-Sun 10am to 12pm The Wig & Pen has just re-opened after a major refurbishment and is Northampton town centres Real Ale pub. There are 8 Real Ales to choose from as well as 2 traditional ciders. A large selection of Belgian beers are aavailable. Food is served daily from 10am-3pm. There is a large garden with an outside bar.
