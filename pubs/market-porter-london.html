Possibly the best pub in London for real ale. They have a massive turnover of beers with some pumps having 2-3 different beers on them each day! The only permanent beer is Harvey's; the remainder are guests from the big regionals as well as the micros. There are always a number of seasonal beers as well. Food is available in their bar as well as an upstairs restaurant but I've no idea what it's like. This is a very busy drum that opens at 6am for the market workers. This is definitely a destination pub-seek it out and enjoy.
