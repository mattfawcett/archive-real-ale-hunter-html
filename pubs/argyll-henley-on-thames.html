The Argyll is a popular Henley market place pub, we are informal and open all day, serving traditional ales, wines and great British home cooked food. Meals available until 9.00pm Monday to Sunday. The pub attracts a wide range of customers from local business people, international tourists, day trippers, locals and shoppers. Henley on Thames is a medieval town dating back to the 11th century with lots to see and do for all the family. There is a covered patio area and also parking at the rear of the pub.
