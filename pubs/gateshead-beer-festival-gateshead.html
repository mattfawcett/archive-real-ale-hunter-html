The Gateshead Beer Festival is a new event organised by Gateshead Rugby Club and hosted over 3 days at their ground in the Low Fell in Gateshead. The event takes place over the first May Bank Holiday this year, Friday 30th April to Sunday 2nd May and combines a Beer Festival of over 40 different beers with entertainment from a range of acts. The Jarrow Brewery, regular suppliers of cask ales to Gateshead RFC, are supporting the event helping to ensure that beers and ciders from around Britain are delivered in top condition for the 3 day event and served to you at their best.
