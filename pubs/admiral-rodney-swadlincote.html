The pub prides itself on being a traditional, friendly village pub offering 5 real ales. The landlord tries to keep at least one hoppy, golden ale, a browner ale and a dark ale always on tap. The Admiral Rodney won the Burton & South Derbyshire District CAMRA pub of the year award in 2007. There is a quiz night on Fridays, karaoke on Sunday, and other seasonal events. The rear of the pub looks out over the Hartshorne Village Cricket pitch where you can relax with a drink and enjoy watching the village team on a warm summer afternoon. The pub serves traditional Sunday lunches. It is advisable to book. They often host a Beer & Rock Festival during August. They also run a Cheese Society Night (As featured on Radio 2's Chris Evans Show) every 1st Monday of the Month at 9:15pm onwards, everyone welcome with their portion of cheese.
