One of the three pubs in the centre of poulton which keep cask beers.The building was once the Poulton town hall,but was sold when Wyre council was formed.This is a busy pub selling four cask beers,always in good condition. The staff are young ,but serve beer as it should be served and are always pleasent and polite. The pub is open plan with the bar at the top end a pool table and darts are able to be played at the bottom end of the pub.If you find yourselfe in Poulton then try the Town Hall i am sure you wiil enjoy the beer and great company.
