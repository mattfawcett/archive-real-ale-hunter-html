This pub owned by the same people as North Bar has a modern interior and is located about 10 minute walk from the Grove so is worth popping in if your down there. They usually have a roosters brew and one pump is dedicated to porters and stouts. They also have a good selection of Belguim and German beers. they serve food 12-4 and 6-10 daily. The pub seems to be getting busier as more and more people move into the new flats that are been developed around the area. There are 2 floors, the main area being downstairs along with a courtyard area at the back and upstairs is usually used for diners although it can be hired out for functions.
