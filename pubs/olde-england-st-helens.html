Olde England is a genuine victorian, real ale pub and free house in the middle of St.Helens town centre on the corner of College Street and Corporation Street by the Landings roundabout. Serving hand pulled bitters, many sourced from local micro breweries, Member of SIBA and 100/100 cask marque score . We also serve an extensive range of, spirits and cocktails, wines and Lyme Bay fruit wines, a range of real ciders, bottled ciders and draught ciders. Olde England also has it's own micro brewery, "Evening Star Brewery", who's brewster is the Olde England licensee "Julia Carmichael". Olde England have beer prices matching or CHEAPER than Wetherpoons, with many special offers. As well as the permanent ales there is always a large selection of real ales some of them new and making their debut serving at the olde England. Beer Garden. Log burning fire, video jukebox, themed nights, pool table, TV and a fantastic clientele.
