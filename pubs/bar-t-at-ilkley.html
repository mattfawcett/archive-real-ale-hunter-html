Bar T'at is a no smoking pub owned by Market Town Taverns. It is located in the small town of Ilkley and has 2 floors and an outside drinking area. The pub used to be a shop before it was converted and was recently doubled in size by extending onto an old derelict garage They serve decent food and have a curry night on Thursdays and Steak Nights on Wednesdays.
