An award winning free house in the heart of Farnborough. We’ve built a reputation for the quality and choice of real ales we offer and when you visit you can select from five regular ales, four guest beers and a great value ‘session’ ale. CAMRA Hampshire Pub of the Year 2009 to 2013 We have an extensive 'home cooked' lunchtime menu cooked to order on the premises, served Monday to Saturday between 12 and 2.00 pm. Drop in and try our delicious Sunday roasts, served between 12 and 4.00 pm every Sunday.
