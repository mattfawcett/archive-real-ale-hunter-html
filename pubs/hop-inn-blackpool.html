This pub is in central Blackpool about five hundred yards from the prom.It is an ex Whitbread managed house sold to Green King recently.The pub gets very busy at weekends with people visiting for hen and bachelor weekends before they go to the night clubs.I sometimes call in during a weekday afternoon when the pub is not as busy and although there is only Green King on sale,i like a pint of Abbot ale.
