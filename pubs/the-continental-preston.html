Overlooking the river Ribble and close to Preston city centre and Avenham and Miller parks, The Continental has for many years been a destination venue for relaxed, family-friendly dining. Recently refurbished after a year's closure the new Continental has opened its doors with a new look, an ever-changing line up of guest ales, a new menu using locally-sourced produce wherever possible and great new facilities for family and entertainment.
