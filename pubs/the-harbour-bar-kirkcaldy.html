Wide selection of guest ales plus ales from the Fyfe brewing Company situated behind the pub. "This is the home of Fife's premier ale house. In the past 13 years it has won Fife's supreme award 11 times. An average of 20 ales appear each week along with 50 malt whiskies." Probably the best choice of ales in any pub in Fife, a must visit if you're in the area.

Added by Dave - almost 10 years ago

Smashing little pub with lots of character and a great choice of ales. On tap that week was Salford Flyer, Trashy Blonde (BrewDog) and two or three others including an ale from the pub's own micro-brewery (The Fyfe Brewing Company.) Friendly barmaid and locals, definitely worth a visit. Only 10-15 minutes walk from bus station and a little longer from the train station.
