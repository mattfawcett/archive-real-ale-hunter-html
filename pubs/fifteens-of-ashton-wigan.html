Harry's Bar is one of Wigan's finest and oldest pubs. If you're from Wigan, you know Harry's, and even if you're not, we wouldn't be surprised if you knew it! Home of The World Pie Eating Championships this boozer is a must visit! Harry's is open every day and serves a fine pint. There is also a great selection of real ales on offer. All of our staff are very friendly, professional, and well mannered, they are more than happy to help you with any of your enquiries. Beer books are a big part of Harry's Bar. The customers never leave home without them! Harry's is big on sports, showing most live games as well as racing and any other sporting events that tickle your fancy!
