Added by BENNETTONBEER - 2012

Visited on 14/9/2011 and once again found the atmosphere of this mock-Tudor Pub to be very welcoming. The friendly staff behind its small bar happily went out of their way to be helpful. A decent wine list and selection of spirits were a fine addition to their well kept Real Ales. When in central Mayfair this Pub is a must.
