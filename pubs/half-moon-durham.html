Opening times Mon - Sat 11am - 11pm, Sun 12pm - 10.30pm 4 pumps with two regular ales and two guest, one usually from Durham Brewery. All are well kept. Several large screens for when the matches are on, has a large outdoor drinking area with plenty benches and tables, which goes right down to the river. Can get quite busy.
