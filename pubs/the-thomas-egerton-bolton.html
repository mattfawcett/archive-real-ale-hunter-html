Enjoying an ideal location in the centre of the busy village of Egerton and surrounded by breathtaking moorland countryside; we hope you will find the Thomas Egerton the perfect place to enjoy superb food cooked to perfection and complimented by an extensive range of cask ales and fine wines. All our dishes are freshly prepared on the premises and great care is taken to ensure that the finest locally sourced ingredients are used. We have an enthusiastic and talented team of chefs who all share a passion for traditional and modern British cuisine and regional recipes and specialities. The specials menu is constantly changing and reflects the best seasonal produce available. We also like to offer everyday dishes at reasonable prices - so check out the blackboard or ask our waiting staff what is available.
