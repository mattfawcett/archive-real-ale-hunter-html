Website: The Calverley Arms was formerly known as The Elmwood Hotel. It is now known in Calverley and across Yorkshire as a fine country pub. A popular pub restaurant with great food, we are situated just North of Pudsey between Leeds and Bradford. There is always a warm welcome with roaring log fires, freshly prepared food, a great selection of real ales and a diverse wine menu for you to choose from.
