This pub is a brew pub, and brews many different brews.The pub supports Bury FC and donates money from certain brews to the club. The pub is in the village of Nangreaves and is in a great place for viewing local towns like Bury Bolton and Ramsbottom from the hills. Food is available daily and the standard like the beer is very good.

Added by BENJO - 2011

Called here for Sunday lunch and a pint,what a great pub and in a great location.Large car park from which you can see for miles.The pub is the home of Leyden Brewery and there were at least six of their brews on the bar. I had the Nanney Flyer 3.8abv named after the small bus that used to take the people of the village to and from Bury market and it realy is a top brew.
