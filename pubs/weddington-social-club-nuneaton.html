Our Guest Ales range from anything from A-Z, at the moment we only serve one guest ale in a 9 gallon barrel so we can change from one to another regularly. At the moment we have Reverent James, then Doombar, then Old Hookey. We rotate these beers on a very regular basis, the previous beers mentioned are not the only ones we sell, ( yes, we also sell Jaipur & many more ) by all means, contact us either by email or telephone to ask which beer we may have on if you are in the vacinity.The Steward of our club, (Brian) will be able to give you a fairly good "Guestimate" of which beer may be on when you arrive!
