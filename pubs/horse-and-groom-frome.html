This is a country pub well off any major road, it is on the border of Wiltshire and Somerset.The pub has two small rooms with a bar serving both of them.There is a conservertory at the end of the pub which is used as a dining room. The meals are very good,and are also very large.All cask beers are served stright from the barrell,even though there are three hand pumps on the bar.
