The Fox & Hounds is part of the Ember Inns chain and has around 8 hand pumps although they usually just serve the regular ales from them and sometimes have a guest. They sometimes hold mini beer festivals where they aim to serve guest beers on their remaining pumps.

Added by matt - 2008

This is the second time I have visited this pub and on the first time I wasn't too impressed as the beer choice was lacking and the quality wasn't too good either but this time there was a beer festival. I was glad to see a reasonable selection of beers and some that you don't really see very often round these parts (Otter for example). I had a pint of Otter and a pint of the Wainright. Both were of very good quality and its definitely worth popping in if your in the area while the festivals on.
