At long last South Devon has a real ale pub to rival such establishment as the Cask Pub & Kitchen in Pimlico. On a much smaller scale perhaps, it opened on the 3rd May 2013 under the direction of joint partner, Beer Guru and Head Chef, Stuart O'Dell. The range of beers that will be available is quite spectacular and accompanied by quality and interesting pub food. Add to this the lower ground floor is a retail beer cellar stocking more than 100 different craft beers from the UK, US, Belgium and elsewhere. This is a must visit for anyone for many miles around.....just check it out and enjoy!

Now moved up the road and now a craft beer bar, the bottle shop is still there but now you can drink in. 4 cask and 6 keg taps serving the best beers from around the world. Good ambience and knowledgable staff who will offer tastings. Food looks great and the scotch eggs are huge!! Great new bar in Newton Abbot and looking to get better!

Added by BENNETTONBEER - 2014

I had the pleasure of attending the invitation only party the day before the Teign Cellars official opening. Wow, was I impressed and not just because of the free food! This is a fantastic opportunity for locals and visitors alike to sample great craft/artisan beers from around the world. The atmosphere was great and the omens appear to be very good. The Teign Cellars is situated on East Street just off the main shopping area, quite close to the Railway Station and Bus stops and not far from that other justly famous watering hole...the Cider Bar. This is a superb pub and the range of bottled and gravity beers complemented those on draught, there being so many I forgot to record them all, well at least that's my excuse! This is certainly a must visit pub...so drink to that
