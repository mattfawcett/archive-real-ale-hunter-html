Welcome To The Twice Brewed Inn We are situated on the B6318 (the Military Road) midway between Newcastle upon Tyne in the East and Carlisle in the West. The Inn is set amongst some of the most breathtaking scenery in England and stands in the shadows of Steel Rigg, without doubt one of the most dramatic parts of Hadrian's Wall. The Twice Brewed is close to all the main Roman sites - Vindolanda, Housteads and The Roman Army Museum.
