Little Fifteens is situated in the heart of Wigan nightlife, a stylish unusual decor with lots for the eye to take in, furniture from around the world, paintings of all varieties to admire and special features throughout such as the love booth and ladies double loos. The relaxed atmosphere is complemented by the choice in background music throughout the day classics such as nina simone, frank Sinatra, early evening more contempory upbeat tracks. Recently awarded the Healthy Eating Award, our menus offer a selection of freshly prepared snacks, starters, pizza and pasta dishes and main meals using local produce using the healthiest cooking practices. Fresh teas and coffee are served throughout the day, using quality coffee beans and top of the range fraccino coffee maker, our staff are trained to serve every latte or cappuccino as their best! Selection of cakes and pastries to die for and cream teas. Specialising in parties we have an amazing vip room called the Sitting Room featuring candelabras and long party tables ideal for small select groups or private parties. We cater for kids by offering a great pizza making party ideal for over 7years. How our week goes ....lunchtimes are bustling with shoppers and workers, early doors great atmosphere people calling in on their way home, weekend nights lively and great for people watching, Saturday afternoons lads watch the football girls do lunch its a nice mix!
