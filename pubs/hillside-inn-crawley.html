Added by Big dug - 2010

Went for some lunch midweek and it had a good trade with diners and drinkers inside and out. Beer was ok but nothing to write home about. Staff were young, friendly and helpful. Food was dire: soggy pastry, soggy veg. Clearly microwave and pre-prepared dishes were there order of the day. Sadly it doesn't live up to the corporate blurb.
