A traditional Welsh Inn in the heart of Criccieth close to the golf club, railway station and adjacent to the main bus stop. Open throughout the year we are popular with walkers and cyclists and visitors to the town who can stop and enjoy a hearty meal of good honest pub food whilst trying some of our range of real ales. We rotate our real ales quite regularly to give everybody an opportunity of finding one they know or trying something new. Some real ales that are on tap regularly are; Greene King IPA, Ruddles Best, Adnams Broadside, Charles Wells Bombardier, Greene King Abbot Ale, Wychwood Hobgoblin, Hook Norton Old Hooky and Morland Old Speckled Hen.
