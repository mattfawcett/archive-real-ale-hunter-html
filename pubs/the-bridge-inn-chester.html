Over a hundred years old The Bridge Inn backs onto the Shropsire Union canal about a mile from Chester City centre. Friendly place to come for a drink. A real open fire and a log burner make this a great place to sit and chat on a winter's evening! Dog's welcome, mooring's for three boats outside.Good quality home cooked food sourced localy.5 ever changing real ales supporting local brewery's.Also real cider on one pump at all times!Live music from local bands every saturday night from 9pm.
