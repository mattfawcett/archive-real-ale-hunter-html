Victorian Two room Pub on the Grand Union Canal in the town of Berkhamsted, Hartfordshire. Locally known as "The Riser" serving locally brewed Real Ale & Cider. It's also on the front Cover of The Good Beer Guide 2012, Biggest selection of spirits for miles around if you fancy a sniffter before you go, over 200 at the last count, very simple food, ie: just Ploughmans, odd opening times see the web site for details on this and forthcoming Guest Ales & Ciders ( www.theriser.co.uk ) or ( www.theriser.net )
