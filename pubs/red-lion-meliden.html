Gordon, Marilyn & Family would like to welcome you to the Red Lion Inn & Restaurant, Meliden. The Red is a traditional pub at the heart of the community offering a great range of beers, wines and a fantastic choice of homecooked food and you can even stay the night in our en-suite bedrooms and enjoy our famous full english breakfast.
