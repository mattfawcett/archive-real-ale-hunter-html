Website: http://www.thegreenmanmiddleton.co.uk/ The Green Man is a traditional village pub & restaurant in Middleton serving tasty, freshly cooked food and top-drawer drinks, with a log-fire when it's chilly and a great beer garden for the warmer weather. You'll find us in the historic village of Middleton, just a few miles from Tamworth to the north and Sutton Coldfield to the southeast, and a short drive from Drayton Bassett, Kingsbury and Wishaw, home of The Belfry golf course.
