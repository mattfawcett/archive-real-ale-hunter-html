The Terrace Bar is open to members of public 365 days per year. Our friendly staff work behind a well stocked bar. Northern's large wine selection and continuously changing real ales are very popular. The Terrace Bar also has a large outside drinking area with outstanding views over the grounds. Which is particularly popular on warmer evenings.
