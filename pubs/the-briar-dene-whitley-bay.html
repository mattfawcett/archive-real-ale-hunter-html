A wonderful real ale pub that is set in a stunning sea side location. This pub by the sea has a wide selection of real ales,wines and soft drinks. Famous for its fresh, locally caught fish and chips this pub is the perfect place to visit any day of the week. As a special treat The Briar Dene's desserts are made at The Cafe Royal bakery, Newcastle's number one artisan bakery.
