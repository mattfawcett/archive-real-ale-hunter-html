The Kings Head Inn, is situated in the wonderful Cotswolds countryside, on the famous Cotswold Way, so why not stop in for lunch or coffee in our brand new Coffee Shop. If you are staying in the Village or close by visit the pub for a wide range of drinks, snacks and meals. The pub boasts a wonderful refurbished restaurant and kitchen with a exciting brand new menu designed by Sean Griffin, Sarah Watson and James Rufus Myer our fantastic new kitchen staff. Our new coffee shop is open from 8:30am and is run by Kerry Greenaway a lovely local village girl, who makes the most wonderful cakes.
