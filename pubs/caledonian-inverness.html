Added by BENJO - 2010

A new pub that must have cost a fortune to convert from the ex bank. We called in for a meal whilst on holiday as we have a Smith and Jones pub in Fleetwood.The pub in Fleetwood is not to clever but is ten times better than this one.The beer was not fit to drink and after waiting over an hour for our food and two complaints to the manager we were offered our money back which we accepted. I thought Wetherspoons were bad but this lot beat them.
