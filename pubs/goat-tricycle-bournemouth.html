Traditional Real Ale Pub, offering 10 hand pulled ales, 5 Wadworth finest, and 5 varying guest ales. The pub is a hidden gem in Bournemouth set back off the main town bustle. With Traditional style and friendly staff it is an enjoyable place to go for a drink and quality food. The menu offers a wonderfull range of light lunches such as crayfish or sausage and mustard baguettes, to fantastic main meals, with homemade pies, beer battered haddock and chips to the famous Goat Curry... and yes it is made with real goat. Opening hours are 12 midday to 11pm monday to friday with food served 12 - 3 and 6 - 9 each day. Friday and saturday nights closing half an hour later at 11:30, and food on a saturday served from 12 through till 8pm. Sunday open 12 midday till 11 pm with food from 12 - 4. Sunday boasts a popular quiz, that is enjoyed by many. If your looking for a traditional real ale pub, full of atmosphere, friendly and welcoming, with an amazing selection of fine ales then you will love the Goat and Tricycle. Once found it is a pub you will keep wanting to go back.
