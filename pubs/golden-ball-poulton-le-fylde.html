A nice pub in the centre of Poulton.The pub has three handpumps,two of which are used for guest beers which are changed two or three times a week.The pub has a very steady trade,and can get very busy at weekend with the younger drinker.The pub is open all day seven days a week with late closing on Friday and Saturday.
