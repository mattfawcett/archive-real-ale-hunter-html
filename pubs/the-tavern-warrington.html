THE TAVERN IS A FUN AND FRIENLY PUB JUST OUTSIDE THE TOWN CENTRE. A BIG MULTI SPORTS MULTI SCREEN VENUE SHOWING ALL LIVE SPORTS MAINLY RUGBY LEAGUE & FOOTBALL & MOTOR SPORTS. 8 HAND PUMPS ON PERMANTLY FROM MICRO & SOMETIMES NATIONAL BREWERIES FROM FAR AND WIDE REGUALAR BEERS FROM ITS OWN BREWERY 4TS NOW BREWING FROM BIGGER SITE IN RUNCORN. ALSO REGULAR BEERS FROM ABBEYDALE, COPPER DRAGON, OAKHAM, TITANIC, THORNBRIDGE TO NAME A FEW. GOOD PRICING WITH SPECIAL OFFERS ON MOST WEEKS ALSO ITS GOT A FANTASTIC RANGE OF BOTTLED BEERS, BELGIUM BEERS, CIDERS, WORLD LAGERS & CONTIENTAL BEERS. LARGE OUTDOOR SPACE WITH COVERED SMOKING AREA. GIVE IT A VISIT IM SURE YOU WILL BE IMPRESSED WITH THE ATMOSPHERE, PRICE AND BEST OF ALL BEER QUALITY.
