The Last Drop was York Brewery's first venture into pubs, and it's still going strong over ten years later! We serve awesome home-made, freshly prepared, and (most importantly) reasonably priced food 12-3, 5-8pm Monday to Friday, and 12-5pm at the weekend. We have live acoustic music from all genres on a Tuesday evening from 9pm, and our quiz runs Wednesday nights from 9pm.
