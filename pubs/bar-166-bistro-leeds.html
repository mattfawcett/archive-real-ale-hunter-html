Open Tuesday - Sunday from 12pm. We have a reputation for serving fresh home cooked food and local beer. we continue to offer this with all the emphasis being on quality. The bar is a welcoming setting for a quick lunch, after work drinks or a night out come the weekend. We have a wide selection of Beers, Craft Ales, Wines & Spirits that are forever growing. Our chefs are continually working to produce daily specials with ingredients from our local suppliers. Our restaurant is ideal for every occasion. We can tailor make menu's and party packages for any number of guests and also have one of Horsforth's only function rooms. Perfect for a Birthday meal, anniversary, wedding's, christenings or any other occasion you can think of however big or small. Children under 8 eat free (1 meal for every 2 paying adults) Any two Lunch items for £12.50 Tuesday Steak Night - steak, chips, glass of wine or beer for only £10
