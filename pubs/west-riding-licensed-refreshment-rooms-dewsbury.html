I think even tumble weed would be a welcome addition to this pub... What a waste. It used to be such a great place to go. Now there is no atmosphere, no regulars & no familiar faces behind the bar anymore. There aren't even as many ale trailers getting through the doors as there used to be. Mexican food???? Really??!!! This was meant to be a real ale pub, last thing anyone wants is a fajita with a pint of best!!! If I wanted to be served and yapped at by kids I'd go to McDonalds... but then again maybe I should as service is better!! Grotty, mismanaged, over priced, sweaty dump. Full of junkies and pot heads. Do yourself a favour and keep on going past this dump or avoid. There are better pubs like the Navigation in Mirfield or Leggers in Savilletown where you get proper beer and a good welcome.
