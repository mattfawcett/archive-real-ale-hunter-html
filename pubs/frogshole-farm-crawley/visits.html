Added by Big dug - 2009

There are now a couple of guest beers appearing on the bar although the range will be limited to whatever agreement the coporate beast has in place with it's supplier. Having said that Black Knight from Wiltshire is not a beer you will come accross in many places in the South. It's a lovely dark, smooth beer with a great taste of roasted malt and some definite hoppy bitterness coming through.

TEA - Hogs Back Brewery

Added by Big dug - 2009

Well run pub, professionally staffed although it can feel a little souless at times. Beer is always on top form; there is clearly a high turnover. The choice is a bit predictable but they are all good beers. There is also a strawberry beer, a wheat beer and Leffe on draught so that shows a bit of committment somewhere in the organisation. Food is also consistently good and keenly priced with plenty of home made choices.
