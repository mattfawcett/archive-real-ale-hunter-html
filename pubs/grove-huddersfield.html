An outstanding pub dedicated to beer. Six permanent ales plus ten guests from all over the UK. Currently the only pub on the UK mainland to have an Irish micro beer as a permanent. Usually stocks between 220-250 British & European bottled beers & ciders. No food but probably the best selection of beers snacks ever ranging from jerky, biltong, crickets to nuts and crisps - not the run of the mill stuff though! No pool table, juke box, bandits etc. Trad Irish session most Thursdays. Perry & cider on hand pump. Beer range is so vast that they have a printed menu for the beer which is also available on the webiste Opening hours 12 midday - 11pm Sunday-Thursday, 12 midday - 12 midnight Friday & Saturday.
