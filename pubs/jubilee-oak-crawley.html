Added by Big dug - over 9 years ago

This pub epitomises all that is wrong with Weatherspoons. It is a cheap, grubby town centre pub catering for the cheap lager crowd. There is never enough staff on duty and the hygiene levels of the kitchen staff are seriously questionable. However......their damn beer is always good. There are usually 3-4 guest beers, sometimes more, and they are always well looked after. It's what makes Weatherspoons so maddening.
