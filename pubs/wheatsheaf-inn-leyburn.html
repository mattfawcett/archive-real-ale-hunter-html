The Wheatsheaf Inn is a traditional Yorkshire Dales accommodation and pub in the village of Carperby. Only a mile or so from Aysgarth Falls and about three miles from Bolton Castle, it is in some of the most beautiful countryside in Yorkshire. The inn has 13 en-suite rooms and the pub serves good cask ales with frequent guest ales. The pub has a good mix of walkers, tourists and locals. It is a friendly, relaxed atmosphere with a restaurant serving good pub food.
