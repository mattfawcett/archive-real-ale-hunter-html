Originally a malthouse Fernades now houses a brewery in the basement, continental Bier keller on the first floor and brewery tap on the second floor. The Brewery Tap is open Mon-Thurs: 4pm-11pm Fri-Sat: 11am-1am Sun: 12 noon - 12 midnight. The Bier keller is open Wed 6.30-11, Thurs 6.30 till 12 Fri & Sat 6.30 till 01.00 Sun 12 till 10.30 11 real ales 2 real ciders/perry draught & bottled continental beers & large selection of jenever gins continental smorgesbords (cheese & meat) available in bier keller The website can be found at

Added by matt - 2009

I only heard about the opening of the new middle bar 'Bier keller' when getting my new copy of the Good Beer Guide 2009 so decided that I had to try it out. The Bier Keller was nice inside and had a good selection of Foreign beers both on tap and in bottle. They just have 1 cask beer in this bar at the moment which is Osset Silver King. I had a pint of Erdinger (the dark one) before we went upstairs. I was suprised to see that the number of hand pumps from what I remember to be around 8 to 11! This is one of my favorite pubs.
