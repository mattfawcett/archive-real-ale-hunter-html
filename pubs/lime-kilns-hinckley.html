The Lime Kilns is a Marston's pub which serves consistently good beer and food. Located on the Ashby Canal, it has a variety of customers so bar talk is never dull! It usually has 2 guest ales on, including Jenning's Cocker Hoop and the less common Marston's Old Empire. There is also regularly a real cider and Marston's Oyster Stout is available (currently only in nitro-keg, although another pump may be added for it soon) Food is home-made and good value. The pub hosts regular beer festivals.
