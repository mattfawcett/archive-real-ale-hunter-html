The White Lion Inn is the hidden gem of the Test Valley and lieas at the centre of the historic, idyllic and river side thatched village of Wherwell. The village is located about 4 miles from Andover and the A303 on the B3420. The Inn serves 5 real ales, ciders and fine wines together with a large selection of soft drinks in order to compliment our varied country classic food menu, specials and our celebrated selection of 9 handmade butter pastry savoury pies. In addition the Inn has 5 bed and breakfast letting rooms and private dining and function room facilities. The Inn boasts a pretty courtyard garden and off road parking for 30 vehicles.
