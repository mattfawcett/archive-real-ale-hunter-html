Added by BENNETTONBEER - 2014

Visited this excellent pub on the Saturday before the Vikings vs Steelers match. The pub was heaving with Steelers fans and American visitors, yet the staff were helpful and friendly. The beer selection was excellent, the food is tremendous and the atmosphere....it was fantastic. So Gary and Maggie who run the pub are certain to see me again.....and again!
