The Clifford Arms is an excellent venue with well kept real ale, good food and Trad Jazz on a Monday and often Modern Jazz on a Sunday. Publican Michael Balster runs a tight ship and provides a professional and warm welcome to visitors and locals alike. Situated in the delightful village of Shaldon it is only a stones throw from the beach and views across the Teign estuary to Teignmouth. The Clifford Arms has much to offer and indeed encourages you to return again, and again
