Large Wetherspoons/Lloyds place in the recently built Walkergate complex. Stocks a good selection of foreign bottled beers. They've cleverly designed this place so even if the music is belting out in the main bar/dance area, if you are round in the seating area (which there is plenty of) you can still hold a conversation. Food is reasonably priced and usually quite good for a Wetherspoons. The smoking area has just recently had to be moved even further out as part of the neighbouring nightclub 'Loveshack' constituted as the 'third' wall, classing it as an enclosed space.

Stuck with the Bombardier, not a bad pint, good earthly taste from the Fuggles. A friend had a problem with a malfunctioning Pedigree, he took it back and got it changed by sympathetic barstaff, apparently it was 'water' in the line as they'd just cleaned them, hmm nice. I ordered the mixed grill, it arrived very quickly and wasn't bad at all, and of course being a 'Spoons' it was cheap as chips, which you also got with the grill.
