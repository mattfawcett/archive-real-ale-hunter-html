The House Without A Name was once 2 cottages, knocked together in approx 1832, with traditional beams and flag flooring. A local, community, pub with a warm and cosy atmosphere. We currently have 6 Cask Ale hand pumps and regularly have 4 permanent ales with one or two Guests. We are on the Finest Cask list and rotate our guest ales weekly. Plans to carry on the August Bank Holiday Beer Festival are afoot so please visit our website at www.HouseWithoutAName.co.uk Why not 'Like' us on facebook - www.facebook.com/HouseWithoutAName Or follow us on Twitter @HouseWithNoName
