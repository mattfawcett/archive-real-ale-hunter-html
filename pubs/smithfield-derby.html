This unusually shaped Public House is home to some great Ales, always in perfect condition and fantastic food (Value 4 Money assured). The spacious patio area creates an excellent atmosphere situated next to the River Derwent with adequate seating and tables for all. During the summer have the ever popular Blues, Booze and BBQ's evenings.
