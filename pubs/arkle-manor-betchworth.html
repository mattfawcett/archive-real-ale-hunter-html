Added by Big dug - over 9 years ago

Visited primarily for lunch and had a superb pint of Landlord; it could not have been in better condition. The only disappointment was that they had 4 handpumps but all with the Landlord pumpclip on them so obviously so immediate plans to increase the range on offer. It's a very large, comfortable, well appointed pub with good staff. Food (good but a little pricey) is its main focus but it does have good seating areas for drinkers. Worth a visit.
