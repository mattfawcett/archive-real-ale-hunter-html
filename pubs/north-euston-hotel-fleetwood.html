The Euston hotel is the largest hotel in Fleetwood,and most of the weddings and parties are held there.There is a large public bar two restraunts and a ballroom,as well as acommadation.The bar has cask beer and has been in the Good Beer Guide but lost it mainly due to change of ownership and changes of bar managers There is live music in the bar at weekends, with a good jazz band on Sunday lunch.
