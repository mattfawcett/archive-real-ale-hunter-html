The Burton Bridge Inn is a traditional real ale pub based in the historic brewing town of Burton On Trent. Linked to the Burton Bridge Brewery, our pub offers award winning ales produced using traditional methods by the brewery. With guest ales, a fine selection of Porters wines, spirits & soft drinks, there is sure to be a drink for everybody. We have a traditional Skittle Alley as well as a meeting room, both of which are available for hire; throughout the year we also host Beer Festivals with the Burton Bridge Brewery.
