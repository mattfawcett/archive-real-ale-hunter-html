Perhaps the greatest pub in the world? A beautiful pub on the banks of the River Clyst in Topsham, just outside of Exeter. It does not appear to have changed for decades. There interior is a couple of small rooms connected by a hallway. There is no recognisable bar. Instead there's a doorway leading down to the cellar where the barman loiters takes orders. Only real ale is served and it's only served by gravity, straight from the firkin. Beers are changed daily. This pub is definitely worth making every effort to visit. I spent a marvelous summer evening here watching the sunset over the river, drinking superbly cared for real ale.
