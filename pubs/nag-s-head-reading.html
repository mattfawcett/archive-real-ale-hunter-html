A constantly changing selection of ales from the regions smaller brewers, There is always at least one stout and one mild. There are premium largers and ciders (including Westons Old Rosie) No Keg bitter, no Guiness, no Stella, no Carling, no Fosters. A Fab wine list all with the same mark-up as beer. Opens 12.00 every day closes 11.00 weekdays and midnight Fridays and Saturdays
