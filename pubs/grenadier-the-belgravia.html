Added by Big dug - almost 10 years ago

Visited 24th February 2009 in the afternoon. The place was heaving with the Law Society Golf Club so we had to watch our wallets. Cracking pub full of character and the dining areas at the back are very atmospheric. There were 3 beers available and the two we tried were on top form. The only downside was the humorless, slow barmaid. She appeared totally bored by the whole experince of being there. I'll definitely give it another go but possibly only once more.
