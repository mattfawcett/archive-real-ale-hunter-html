Opened only in 2011, BrewDog's flag-ship bar in London is a wonderland full of innovative, powerful and tasty beers. BrewDog attract a young and intelligent following of those who choose to reject the usually bland offerings of the major international drinks companies,along with the often tame, rather safe, products of many British craft brewers. BrewDog Camden is on numerous bus routes and only about 100m from Camden Town underground. It is a must visit for for every REAL Real Ale drinker!

Added by BENNETTONBEER - over 6 years ago

Visited this amazing pub on the 6th October and was very quickly won over by both the atmosphere and the amazing range of BrewDog beers, both on draught and bottled. There were numerous guest ales from breweries such as Evil Twin and Rogue, and the ability to try tasters of BrewDog's own infamous ales such as TACTICAL NUCLEAR PENGUIN at 32% and SINK THE BISMARK at 41% was a real joy. For anyone who likes the 'real stuff', not the bland and the safe usually on offer in British pubs, the BrewDogCamden is a must visit.
