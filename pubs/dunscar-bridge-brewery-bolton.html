The brewery consists of a 4.5 barrel brewery located at The Brewhouse and a 25 barrel brewery located at our main offices and warehouses on Dunscar Business Park. The small brewery has been successfully trading since 2009 and the main 25 barrel brewery is currently being installed. The company is a member of both CAMRA and SIBA. We offer brewery tours, merchandise and private hire pumps and kegs.
