This is a student bar, recently exploring into the likes of real ale. They have recently added another 2 hand pumps making a total of 6 pumps now, 3 of which are usually a Black Sheep beer There is good, friendly bar staff, cheap food on hand and decent music. It is free to get in, you just need to have an NUS card after 7 ish, or get someone to sign you in.
