Its rare to have a members club that has a good selection of well kept ales, but Guiseley Factory Workers Club has just that. There are 2 rooms, the main bar when you walk in and a concert room to the back where if you are unlucky you may have to watch the 'turn'. There are usually about 2 guests and they have just had their first beer festival. As its a members club you may have to show a Camra card or a copy of the Good Beer Guide to get in.
