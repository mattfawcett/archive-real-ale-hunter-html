The White Horse Inn is situated in the Shropshire Hills Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty. We serve five local real ales as regulars on our hand pumps including ale from our own micro-brewery. Bed and Breakfast is available and we serve range of home made pub meals. Check out our website for more information.
