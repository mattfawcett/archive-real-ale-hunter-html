Visits for Fernandes Brewery Tap

Add a visit

Added by matt - 2009

I only heard about the opening of the new middle bar 'Bier keller' when getting my new copy of the Good Beer Guide 2009 so decided that I had to try it out. The Bier Keller was nice inside and had a good selection of Foreign beers both on tap and in bottle. They just have 1 cask beer in this bar at the moment which is Osset Silver King. I had a pint of Erdinger (the dark one) before we went upstairs. I was suprised to see that the number of hand pumps from what I remember to be around 8 to 11! This is one of my favorite pubs.
