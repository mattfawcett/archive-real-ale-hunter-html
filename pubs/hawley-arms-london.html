The Hawley Arms has long been famed as a celebrity hang-out and a centre for the music business, and quite deservedly too. Following the huge fire in February 2008 that destroyed the Hawley Arms and much of Camden Market, this famous old pub has been lovingly restored and is once again a vibrant drinking and music venue. Tucked away just off Camden High Street-Chalk Farm Road area, the Hawley Arms is well worth a visit and its admittedly small number of Real Ales are however well kept and regularly changed.

Visited the Hawley Arms on the 25th May 2011 and was immediately impressed by the welcoming and exciting atmosphere. The Bar staff were excellent, as were the three Real Ales on offer. I highly recommend an evening or three spent happily supping good beer and spoting celebrities.
