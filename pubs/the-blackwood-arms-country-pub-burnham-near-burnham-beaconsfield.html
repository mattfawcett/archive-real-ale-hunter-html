The Blackwood Arms Country Pub is situated on the Berkshire/Buckinghamshire border and between the towns of Burnham, Beaconsfield, Wooburn Common and Farnham Common.It is on the edge of Burnham Beeches and sits directly on the Beeches Way - a popular long distance walk. The pub is well known as a real ale pub attracting real drinkers from near and far. We always serve Brakspear and at least 4 other guest ales at any one time. There is also always a proper cider on tap - currently Addlestones Cloudy Still. The pub is in an idyllic location with a great pub garden, log fire and serving good traditional English pub food. The pub food is cooked to order using quality fresh and local produce. We are a dog friendly pub and love muddy boots! A good locals crowd and friendly staff make a popular destination. Our website provides all the information you need plus details of food and drink offers plus regular events. A great place to be.
