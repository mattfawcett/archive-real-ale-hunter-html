A pub close to the seafront which can get very busy at lunchtime with shoppers and visitors.Once a Whitbread managed house it is now owned by Greene King. The pub serves food daily upto eight pm,and seems to do well. On friday and saturday nights the pub gets crowded with young drinkers who seem to use the pub as a meeting place before going to the nightclubs.
