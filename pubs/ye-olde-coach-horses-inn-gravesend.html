Is a local pub a local pub anymore? Nowadays most of them probably aren't what you think of as an old fashioned 'local'. Ye Olde Coach & Horses Inn is, comfortable, atmospheric with an excellent bar and cellar serving award winning real ales. Come in for a quick drink on your way home, try the lunchtime and evening meals (May 2014) or sit yourself by the open fire and relax for a couple of hours. This 'local' gives a warm welcome and invites you to spend a while taking in its friendly charm.
