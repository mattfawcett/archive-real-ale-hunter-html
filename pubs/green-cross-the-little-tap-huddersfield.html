Closed.... Now a Swingers Club!!!!! A traditional looking pub with a really nice feel. Six cask ales, five of which are guest ales, three European Biers and one draft cider. The entertainment consists of a quiz night and live entertainment most other nights. A selection of cold sandwiches and pie and peas are available at weekends. The outside decked area is a great sun trap a place to relax. opening times: mon -thurs 4pm-11pm fri & sat 12pm-11pm sun 12pm - 10.30
