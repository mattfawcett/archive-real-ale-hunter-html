Mr Grundys is a traditional styled pub serving a variety of real ales and lagers. The pub itself has a very relaxed atmosphere with roaring open fires in the winter. In the summer, customers can take advantage of our vast (for an inner city pub!)beer garden and canopied courtyard area. Mr Grundys also serves food everyday, where customers can choose off our pub menu or try something different of the specials boards. Parking is available at the rear of the building and free Wifi is available on request.
