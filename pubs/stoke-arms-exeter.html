Added by BENNETTONBEER - over 7 years ago

I had the misfortune to visit this pub on the 15/7/2011. There is little more to say....except avoid this place at all costs. Two pumps unused and a third with DOOMBAR. This was cloudy, warm and OFF....the worst single half pint of Real ale I have EVER been served. One look, one sniff and one tiny sip was enough before it was returned. The lack of the correct positive response suggests sadly yet another pub destined for an early closure.
