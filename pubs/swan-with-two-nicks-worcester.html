City Centre Pub dating back to 1550 with 4 everchanging cask ales all priced at £1.85 regardless of OG. Also have a large selection of malt whisky's bottled beers and cider. serving food all day, great value pie and a pint. Large selection of wines. Also sell tpigs and good priced coffee. Has a rear bar called Drummonds which on the weekend has live music gets very busy so get there early. upstairs there is a cocktail bar called the Lunar bar very much a retro bar only open weekends There is a very large patio area at the rear of the building with many interesting plants. A must visit venue pub.
