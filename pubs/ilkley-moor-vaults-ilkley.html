The pub has two bars - each with a real fire. The pub is open from 11.30pm and serve lunch from 12pm and dinner from 6pm Monday - Saturday. On Sundays they open at 12pm and Sunday Lunch is available until 3pm. They have daily changing menus of fresh, simple, seasonal food. Everything is sourced locally where possible. There are regularly changing guest ales as well as a good selection of wine Walkers, cyclists, familes and dogs are very welcome and the vaults is an entirely smoke-free venue.
