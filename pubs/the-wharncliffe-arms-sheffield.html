“Jeana and Simon would like to give all our customers a warm welcome here at the Wharncliffe Arms. We are a family business who run a traditional pub with present day values. We welcome new and old customers to try our range of hand pull real ales alongside our regular lagers and beers. The Wharncliffe has been a pub since the 1830's, reopened as a free house in Sept 2012. A long single room split into distinct areas by the bar, open fires at both ends, dart board and tv at one end. A locals pub with a focus on conversation and casual dining including fresh homemade Sunday lunches. Various entertainment on Saturdays, either our resident Karaoke Dj or live singers/bands. (Check website for details) Local carols Sunday lunchtimes through December. Also hold fairly regular charity auctions.”
