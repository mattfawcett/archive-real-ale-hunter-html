Simply one of the best Real Ale public houses in the UK, let alone London. CAMRA North London Branch pub of the year 2009-2010, with 7 real ales on the pump and 11 more on gravity, plus 10 ciders and perrys, and award winning pies. A real dream of a pub where Craig and Karen and their staff make everyone welcome.

Visited the Bree Louise on the 6th October 2010. If its possible this pub just gets better. A great selection of Real Ales, Ciders and Perrys, with an expanding menu of great pub food. The Ciders included BIDDENDEN ORIGINAL, ORCHARD PIG and WESTONS OLD ROSIE
