Added by TheOldCrown - almost 7 years ago

We visited on the 8th April for there Garden party. The new beer garden is amazing and has obviously had a lot of money invested in to it. There are a good selection of real ales on and the Hobgoblin was especially nice, Served in a very nice Hobgoblin glass. They have a pie menu thats pretty darn good with some that you would not normally think of trying. They are award winning for a reason. They also have a normal pub menu which my wife had which was nice. They had a live band which had a great interaction with the crowd. The staff was very friendly and helpfull. All in all a lovely pub in a picturesque location.
