The Sair Inn is a brew pub, serving beers from Linfit brewery at the rear.The pub has four rooms and a bar with open fires.The pub has good local trade, plus many visitors as most Camra trips pay a visit when in the area.The beers brewed by Linfit are mild at 3.0abv, bitter 3.7abv, Gold medal 4.1abv, stout 4.1 abv, Leadboiler 6.8 abv, and Enoch`Hammer at 8.6 abv, plus endless other brews.Most of our trips to this area seem to end here as nobody wants to leave. OPENING TIMES MON-FRI 5pm-11pm SAT 12pm-11pm SUN 12pm.10-30pm
