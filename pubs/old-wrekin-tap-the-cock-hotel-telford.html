The bar area at the Old Wrekin Tap is all wooden with hops hanging from the ceiling and a real fire in the corner, There is a non smoking room at the front. There are no dukebox's or televisions in the bar and non smoking room. There are regular guest beers, infact there is only 1 permament real ale with a further 7 changing regularly. There is always a cider, mild and stout on as a guest. The Tap has won many Camra awards for its beer.
