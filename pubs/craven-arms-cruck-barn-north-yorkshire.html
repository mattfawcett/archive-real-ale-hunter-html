The pub you always hoped to find. The Craven Arms, Appletreewick is nestled in the heart of wharfedale with that 'something' extra special. Serving real ales and great food accompanied by gas lighting, real log fires and a great, friendley atmosphere. The cruck barn behind provides the venue for many large events such as weddings, birthdays, anniversaries and party's. The heather thatched roof and soaring oak trusswork provides a truly unique atmosphere for your celebrations.
